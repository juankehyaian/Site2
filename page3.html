Travis Scott x Air Jordan 1 OG Low

The Jordan 1 Retro Low OG SP "Reverse Mocha" is a collaboration between Jordan Brand and rapper Travis Scott, released in July 2022. Inspired by the success of their previous collaboration, the Jordan 1 High 'Mocha', it features a reverse design, combining white leather with mocha suede and Scott's signature inverted Swoosh. A kids' size retails for $219.
